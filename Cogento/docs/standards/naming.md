# Cogento - Naming Decision

## Official Name

**Cogento** - Self-hosted membership management

## Why Cogento Works

### Pronunciation & Flow

- **Pronunciation:** Co-gen-to (flows smoothly off the tongue)
- **Length:** Short and memorable (3 syllables)
- **Rhythm:** Similar to Imagento/Progento pattern

### Meaning & Branding

- **"Co" prefix:** Flexible - can mean Community, Collective, Collaborative, or Company
- **Appeal:** Works for sports clubs, nonprofits, associations, community groups
- **Professional:** Modern, polished, complete-sounding name

### Pattern Consistency

- ✅ Follows Edgible naming pattern (Co + gento)
- ✅ Consistent with Imagento (Image + gento)
- ✅ Consistent with Progento (Pro + gento)
- ✅ Easy to remember and associate with Edgible ecosystem

## Elevator Pitch

**"Cogento is a free, self-hosted membership management system that uses Stripe as source of truth, with PostgreSQL sync for fast queries and reporting. Perfect for sports clubs, nonprofits, and community groups that want full control without monthly SaaS fees."**

## Key Messages

- ✅ **FREE** - Self-hosted, no monthly fees
- ✅ **Stripe-powered** - Professional payment processing
- ✅ **Self-hosted** - Full control, no vendor lock-in
- ✅ **Fast queries** - PostgreSQL sync for reporting
- ✅ **Open source** - Adapt, extend, contribute
